Work Product Kind: Process
These work products identify the various process or governance related artifacts, such as guidelines, procedures, templates, processes, standards, and policies, that determine how project tasks are, or are suggested, to be completed.
Relationships
Work Products
  • Development Process
Main Description

These work products help everyone on the project know how to do their job effectively and efficiently, or they may be required to comply with either external regulatory bodies or internal business rules. Some are mandatory and are subject to audit, while others may be merely recommended, such as project guidelines.

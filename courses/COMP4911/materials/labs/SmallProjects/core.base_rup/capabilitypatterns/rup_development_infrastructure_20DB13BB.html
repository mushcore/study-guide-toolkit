Work Product Descriptor (Artifact): Development Infrastructure
This work product includes the hardware and software, such as computers and operating systems, on which the tools run. The Development Infrastructure also includes the hardware and software used to interconnect computers and users.
Purpose

A standard development infrastructure exists to enable the development effort to take place.

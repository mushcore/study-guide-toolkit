Training with Rational University
IBM Rational software training provides a blended curriculum of Instructor-Led Training (ILT) and Web-Based Training (WBT) to suit your education needs. Get foundational and prerequisite knowledge through our library of online WBT courses and then extend your knowledge and increase your understanding through in-depth ILT classroom courses.
Main Description

What is Rational University?

IBM Rational software training complements our comprehensive software development solution by combining a proven software development process and best practices within a structured professional education curriculum. Our curriculum, produced by Rational University, includes courses covering all the software development capabilities - requirements and analysis, design and construction, software quality, software configuration management, process and project management, and more.

RUP-Related Training

Rational University offers both Web-based and Instructor-led courses for the Rational Unified Process® product. For a complete list of training, see Rational software training.

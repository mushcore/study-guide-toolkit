#### Four dimensions

| Dimension | Poles | Traits |
| --- | --- | --- |
| E / I (attitude to world) | Extraversion / Introversion | E: face-to-face, think out loud. I: reflective, written comm. |
| S / N (perception) | Sensing / Intuition | S: details, facts, practical. N: patterns, new complex problems. |
| T / F (judging function) | Thinking / Feeling | T: analytical, objective. F: fair, value-driven. |
| J / P (outer-world lifestyle) | Judging / Perceiving | J: decide quickly, schedules. P: enjoy surprises, open. |

#### Functions vs attitudes

-   **Perceiving functions**: Sensing + Intuition (gather info)
-   **Judging functions**: Thinking + Feeling (decide)
-   **Attitudes**: Extraversion/Introversion (toward world); Judging/Perceiving (toward outer lifestyle)

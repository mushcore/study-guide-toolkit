#### Four categories (Steve McConnell)

-   **People**: heroics, motivation issues, insufficient training, friction
-   **Process**: optimistic schedules, no risk mgmt, shortchanged QA, silver-bullet thinking
-   **Product**: requirements gold-plating, feature creep, over-engineering
-   **Technology**: silver-bullet syndrome, switching tools mid-project, inadequate legacy management

#### Disaster Recovery (LOW priority)

Backup + DR planning, business continuity, RTO (Recovery Time Objective), RPO (Recovery Point Objective). Off-site backups. DR tests.

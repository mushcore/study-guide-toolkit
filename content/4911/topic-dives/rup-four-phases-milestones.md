| Phase | Milestone | Focus | Effort |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Inception** | LCO (Life-Cycle Objectives) | Vision, business case, scope, feasibility | ~10% |
| **Elaboration** | LCA (Life-Cycle Architecture) | Architecture baselined, ~80% requirements, major risks mitigated | ~30% |
| **Construction** | IOC (Initial Operating Capability) | Build, integrate, test | ~50% |
| **Transition** | PR (Product Release) | Deploy, UAT, training, handoff | ~10% |

#### Iteration = mini-waterfall

Each iteration includes all workflows (req → analysis → design → impl → test). Number of iterations varies per phase: Inception 1-2, Elaboration 2-3, Construction 3-5, Transition 1-2.

#### "Hump chart" — workflow distribution

-   Requirements hump: peaks in Inception/Elaboration
-   Analysis & Design hump: peaks in Elaboration
-   Implementation hump: peaks in Construction
-   Test hump: peaks in Construction, continues through Transition
